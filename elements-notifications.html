Notifications

Partner comes equipped with advanced page notifications that allow you to show content to users without disrupting their view of the entire page.

Similar to modals, these notifications can be either shown automatically after a certain time or they can be triggered by a button that has the a [data-notification-link] attribute.

Example uses:
  • Cookies policy notifications (essential for EU-based websites)
  • Triggering newsletter signups
  • Alerting users to a promotion or special offer
